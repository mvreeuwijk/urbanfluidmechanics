## 1. Main site pages

### 1.1 /home
- **Title:** Urban Fluid Mechanics Special Interest Group
- **Purpose:** Landing page for a UK-based research community on fluid dynamics in urban environments, funded by the UK Fluids Network. Group meets twice annually; members from universities, industry, and government.
- **Body content:**
  - Welcome section: the SIG addresses "a broad range of issues associated with flows in urban areas"; members pursue fundamental and applied research using experimental, numerical, and theoretical methods.
  - Four Challenge Areas:
    - **C1:** Very rough boundary layers and flow prediction with large obstacles
    - **C2:** Stratification and buoyancy effects on airflow, air quality, and comfort
    - **C3:** Scalar dispersion (passive and active) in urban boundary layers
    - **C4:** Building-scale fluid dynamics — flows around structures and pedestrian effects
  - Upcoming meeting note: 13th meeting, December 16, 2025, at Imperial College London.
- **Links:**
